Got Mushroom?

Data Visualizations

Welcome to our Mushroom Classifier Showcase! At the intersection of technology and nature, our cutting-edge machine learning model has been trained to tackle a critical question: "Is this mushroom safe to eat?" We are excited to present a series of data visualizations that not only demonstrate the incredible predictive power of our model but also shed light on the intricacies of the dataset it was trained on. These visualizations provide insights into how our model assigns weights to different features to determine the edibility of mushrooms, as well as uncover any potential biases in the data. Join us on a journey into the world of mushrooms, machine learning, and data transparency.
